Project Overview


In a semester-long project, I played a pivotal role on a team where we created an app prototype beginning from the user research phase. This data-driven foundation allowed us to meticulously craft user flows, develop storyboards, and map out comprehensive user journeys. For a detailed summary of our work download the final report.



Promotional Poster


I designed a promotional poster on Canva to introduce the ConServe application. The focus was on addressing the issue of food waste in the US and at home. The poster outlined our approach, touching on research, ideation, and prototyping. Finally I added a section that featured our solutions, accompanied by a brief description and iPhone mockups.
